Pumps We Service

Water Tank Pumps

Water tank pumps efficiently transfer water from storage tanks to homes or irrigation systems. Jet pumps are ideal for shallow wells and surface water, providing reliable pressure. Multistage pumps handle high pressure over long distances, while variable speed drive pumps maintain consistent pressure, adjusting speed for energy efficiency.

Pressure Boosting Pumps

Pressure boosting pumps enhance system water pressure, ensuring stable flow. Variable speed models adjust to water demand, saving energy and reducing wear. These pumps are ideal for homes with multiple bathrooms, large families, or areas with low water pressure.

Water Main Changeover Systems

Water main changeover systems like Acquasaver, Rain Main, and Rain Saver optimise water usage by automatically switching between mains water and rainwater based on availability. These systems reduce water bills and promote sustainable water management by maximising rainwater use for irrigation and toilet flushing.

Toilet Pumps

Toilet pumps are essential for efficient wastewater management in areas with low gravity flow. They ensure smooth and reliable operation, preventing blockages and maintaining proper sanitation. Ideal for homes, offices, and commercial buildings, toilet pumps enhance the overall plumbing system by effectively handling waste disposal.
